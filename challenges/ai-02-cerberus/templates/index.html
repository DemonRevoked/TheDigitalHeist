Operation CERBERUS

Cerberus is a black-box AI system used by a covert agency to classify digital activity.

You cannot see its code, model, or training data.
You can only interact with it through its APIs.

Your mission is to observe, understand, and expose Cerberus.

Available Interfaces

POST /analyze

Content-Type: application/json

{
  "features": [ <20 numerical values> ]
}

Returns a classification label, confidence, and probability distribution.

POST /report

{
  "model_params": { ... }
}

Use this only when you believe you understand how Cerberus works internally.

Cerberus always responds.
Whether you understand why it responds the way it does
is the real challenge.

Good luck.
